Crop Diseases Information

Crop Disease Type Identification Signs Solutions
Wheat Rust Yellow to orange pustules on leaves, stem, or spikes Plant resistant varieties, apply fungicides if necessary, practice crop rotation
Powdery Mildew White powdery patches on leaves and stems Improve air circulation, avoid overhead irrigation, apply fungicides as needed
Rice Blast Brown to white lesions on leaves, stem, or panicles Plant resistant varieties, manage water and nitrogen levels, apply fungicides if necessary
Brown Spot Circular brown spots with yellow halos on leaves Rotate crops, improve drainage, manage weeds, use disease-resistant varieties
Maize Gray Leaf Spot Grayish-brown lesions with yellow margins on leaves Rotate crops, practice proper tillage, use disease-resistant hybrids, apply fungicides if necessary
Southern Corn Rust Orange to reddish-brown pustules on leaves and stems Plant resistant varieties, use fungicides preventively, manage crop residues, practice crop rotation
Soybean Soybean Rust Small brown pustules on leaves, later turning into rust-colored spores Plant resistant varieties, apply fungicides preventively, manage plant density and spacing
Soybean Cyst Nematode Stunted growth, yellowing, and wilting of plants Rotate crops with non-hosts, use resistant varieties, manage soil pH and fertility, apply nematicides
Tomato Early Blight Dark concentric rings with yellowing on leaves Remove infected plant parts, mulch to reduce soil splash, apply copper-based fungicides, practice crop rotation
Late Blight Water-soaked lesions turning dark brown on leaves and stems Improve air circulation, avoid overhead irrigation, use resistant varieties, apply fungicides preventively
Potato Late Blight Water-soaked lesions turning dark brown to black on leaves Remove infected plant parts, avoid excessive nitrogen, apply fungicides preventively, practice crop rotation
Potato Wart Warty growths on tubers, often with pinkish or brownish discoloration Use certified disease-free seed potatoes, practice crop rotation, sanitize equipment and storage facilities
Cotton Verticillium Wilt Wilting of leaves, yellowing, and premature defoliation Use resistant cotton varieties, rotate crops with non-hosts, manage soil moisture and fertility, apply fungicides
Fusarium Wilt Wilting of leaves, vascular discoloration in stems Plant resistant varieties, practice crop rotation with non-hosts, improve soil drainage, use soil solarization
Sugarcane Smut Black, powdery masses of spores on stems Plant disease-free seed cane, treat seed cane with hot water, practice sanitation, apply fungicides if necessary
Red Rot Red discoloration and decay of inner tissues in stalks Plant disease-free seed cane, practice crop rotation, manage water and fertilizer application, apply fungicides
Lettuce Downy Mildew Yellow to light-green patches on leaves, fuzzy gray growth Improve air circulation, avoid overhead irrigation, apply fungicides preventively, practice crop rotation
Bottom Rot Soft, water-soaked lesions at the base of leaves Avoid excessive moisture, maintain proper plant spacing, use resistant lettuce varieties, apply fungicides preventively
Carrot Carrot Rust Fly Yellow or brown scars on roots, tunnels inside carrots Use row covers to prevent fly infestation, rotate crops, practice sanitation, apply insecticides if necessary
Alternaria Leaf Blight Brown to black lesions with concentric rings on leaves Remove infected plant debris, improve air circulation, use disease-resistant carrot varieties, apply fungicides
